Behind the Lens
Braden Auerbach

I'm a first-year student passionate about marketing, philosophy, and the art of visual storytelling. For nearly two years, I've been channeling that into a photography business — one frame at a time.

I've shot on the sidelines of nearly 10 different sports, worked with 10 couples on everything from casual sessions to a full wedding last summer, and delivered over 60 professional headshots. Whether you need a sharp LinkedIn portrait or want a big moment frozen forever, I bring the same focus and care to every shoot.
